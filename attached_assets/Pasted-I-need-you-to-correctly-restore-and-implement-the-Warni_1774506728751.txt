IMPORTANT CONCEPT

Warning is:
- a geographic danger area on the map
- used for hazards like gas leak, fire, smoke, chemical risk, unsafe direction, etc.

Warning is NOT:
- an alert notification
- an alert message
- a normal zone

Alert sends notifications.
Warning does not send notifications.
Warning is only visual + geographic + detection logic.
